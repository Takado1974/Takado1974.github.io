# Fees and Price List

Takado ry uses myClub for managing membership data, events, and invoicing. Membership and training fees are paid through [Takado's myClub](https://takado.myclub.fi/).

#### Membership Fee:
* 30 € / calendar year

#### BJJ Adults:
* Basic Course (3 months) 120 € (20% student discount)
* Year (365 days) 580 €
* 6 Months (180 days) 320 €
* 1 Month (30 days) 60 €
* 10 Sessions 80 €

#### BJJ Juniors and Youth:
* Autumn or Spring Season (Koalas) 110 €
* Autumn or Spring Season (Pandas and Youth) 150 €
* Parent-Child Group 50 € / Autumn or Spring Season (Included in the training fee for those with a valid 1-year or 6-month training fee for any Takado discipline).

#### EJJ and Escrima:
* Year (365 days) 290 €
* 6 Months (180 days) 160 €
* 1 Month (30 days) 30 €
* 10 Sessions 80 €

Everyone training at Takado must pay the annual membership fee.

With a BJJ training fee, you can also participate in EJJ and Escrima training.

A junior refers to a practitioner under the age of 16 who participates in training sessions designed for juniors. Currently, Takado only has junior activities in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ). Juniors have a summer break in June, July, and August. Additionally, junior groups observe holidays according to school break schedules.

Practitioners aged 7-17 can apply for seasonal fee support from the SOS Children's Villages "Unelmista totta" (Dreams Come True) hobby support system at www.unelmista.fi.

In junior groups, only a 50% sibling discount is applicable.

You can use the following exercise benefits for training fees: Smartum, Edenred, Eazybreak, ePassi, and TYKY vouchers. Exercise benefits cannot be used for the membership fee. Exercise benefits are personal and cannot be used to pay for a family member's or another person's training fees.

You can also use a Smartum Gift Card for training fees if you want to give the joy of exercise as a birthday gift to a loved one, for example!

If you want to start training and become a member of Takado ry, you must register via myClub. Registration can be done through the myClub icon at the bottom of the page or the link above. From the same link, you can also log in and request a password and username reset link to your email if you have forgotten your credentials.

The club recommends that practitioners acquire insurance that covers training and, if necessary, competitions.
