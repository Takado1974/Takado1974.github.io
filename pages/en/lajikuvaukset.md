## Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ)
![Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu](images/lajit_bjj.jpg)
Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport that focuses on grappling and especially ground fighting. The goal is to gain a dominant position over an opponent and end the match using various joint locks or chokes. The absence of strikes and kicks makes it a very safe and versatile hobby for people of all ages.

## European Jujutsu
![European Jujutsu](images/lajit_ejj.jpg)
European Jujutsu is a versatile self-defense and martial art that combines strikes, kicks, throws, locks, and chokes. it provides a comprehensive set of tools for various self-defense situations and develops physical fitness and coordination.

## Escrima
![Escrima](images/lajit_escrima.jpg)
Escrima is a Filipino martial art that focuses particularly on armed combat, such as the use of sticks and knives. Training develops excellent eye-hand coordination, speed, and the ability to use everyday objects for self-defense. Escrima also includes unarmed techniques based on the same principles of movement as armed techniques.
