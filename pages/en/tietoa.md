# About the Organization

Takado ry is a non-profit association that offers training in traditional martial arts for children and adults. Takado's disciplines are European jujutsu, Brazilian jujutsu, escrima (Sikado), and kyudo. Takado is one of the first — if not the very first — Finnish clubs to take up serious training in Brazilian jujutsu. Training runs almost daily throughout the year, and the club organizes camps and seminars for its members each year related to discipline training.
