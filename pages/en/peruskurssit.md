# Basic Courses

Takado regularly organizes basic courses in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), European Jujutsu (EJJ), and Escrima.

In the basic course, you will learn the fundamentals of the discipline safely and under guidance. No prior experience in martial arts is required.
