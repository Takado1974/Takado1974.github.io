# Gym Rules

To ensure that training is pleasant and safe for everyone, we follow these rules:

1. **Cleanliness and Hygiene**: Ensure your training uniform is clean. Keep your nails short and wash your hands/feet before training.
2. **Respect**: Respect the instructors and your training partners. Bowing when entering and leaving the mat is part of the tradition.
3. **Safety**: Follow the instructor's instructions precisely. If you need to stop training due to injury or another reason, inform the instructor.
4. **Equipment**: Remove jewelry, watches, and piercings before training for safety reasons.
5. **Behavior**: No shouting or inappropriate language in the gym. Keep mobile phones on silent in the dressing room.

Together we create a great atmosphere!
