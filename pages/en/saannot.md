# Gym Rules

Takado has shared rules that ensure effective, pleasant, and safe training for everyone.

**Respect others.**
At Takado, everyone is equal, and we do not accept discrimination, harassment, or bullying. Remember good manners, aim to arrive on time, be present, and respect the instruction.

**Train so that no one gets injured.**
Always consider the safety of your training partner and others present.

**Participate in training only when healthy.**
You should not participate in training if you have a contagious disease, such as a cold or skin symptoms. A minor injury does not necessarily prevent training; discuss the matter with the instructor.

**Take care of good personal hygiene.**
Come to training clean and in clean, intact equipment. Keep your nails short and remove jewelry before stepping onto the mat.

**Leave outdoor shoes in the entrance.**
On the mat, we are bare-footed with clean feet; elsewhere in the gym, indoor footwear is used.

**Our association operates on a voluntary basis; do your part.**
Clean up after yourself: trash in the bin, wipe away blood and other stains, and return the equipment you used to its place. If you notice any deficiencies, tell the instructor or the gym manager.

**Do not leave your equipment at the gym.**
Takado ry is not responsible for items left at the gym.

**Clean and protect wounds and bruises.**
The gym's first aid cabinet contains wound care supplies and bandages, but every practitioner should also be prepared with their own supplies.

**Children have their own supervised sessions, but if you bring children to adult training, you are responsible for their safety.**
Children should not be left unsupervised or allowed onto the same training area where a session is in progress.
